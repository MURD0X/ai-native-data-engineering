# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This is a technical project. Contributions are judged on clarity, correctness, and usefulness — not on who submits them.

Expected behavior:
- Give specific, actionable feedback on PRs
- Ask questions when something is unclear before assuming bad intent
- Credit prior art when your pattern builds on existing work
- Keep discussion focused on the technical merits

Not acceptable:
- Personal attacks or dismissive language
- Gatekeeping ("that's not real data engineering")
- Spam or off-topic promotion

## Enforcement

Issues that violate these standards will be closed. Repeated violations result in being blocked from the repo.

## Contact

Open an issue or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.
